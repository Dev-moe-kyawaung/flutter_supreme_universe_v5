# 👥 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender, experience, education, nationality, appearance, religion, or sexual identity.

We pledge to act in ways that create an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior:

- ✅ Using welcoming language
- ✅ Being respectful of viewpoints
- ✅ Accepting constructive criticism
- ✅ Focusing on community best
- ✅ Showing empathy

### Unacceptable Behavior:

- ❌ Sexualized language or imagery
- ❌ Trolling or insulting comments
- ❌ Personal or political attacks
- ❌ Public or private harassment
- ❌ Publishing private information
- ❌ Other inappropriate conduct

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders clarify and enforce standards, taking fair corrective action for violations.

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction

**Impact:** Inappropriate language

**Consequence:** Private written warning, public apology

### 2. Warning

**Impact:** Single violation

**Consequence:** Warning, no interaction with involved people temporarily

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Impact:** Serious violation

**Consequence:** Temporary ban from community interaction

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Impact:** Repeated violations

**Consequence:** Permanent ban from community
